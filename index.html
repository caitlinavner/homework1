About Me

Not too long ago, in a galaxy not too far away, I found myself leaving the dry and arid deserts of Southern California in search of lush scenery and rainy days. Accompanied by my trusty pup, Princess Leia, and my very own ninja/Kung Fu master boyfriend, we found ourselves setting up base here in the Pacific Northwest seeking adventure, intrigue and unexpected journeys. I'm looking forward to wibbly wobbly timey wimey stuff and know that no power in the 'verse can stop me, for the odds are ever in my favor. I solemnly swear that I am up to no good. This is the Captain, signing off now...Mischief managed!'
